ACM for Professionals

ACM is all about using the best of search engine techniques. Search is already integral to our lives, so ACM seeks to apply these techniques in a way that can help us analyse how to pursue that dream job better.



The Situation

A survey posted on AOL.Jobs revealed that over 50 percent of respondents regretted having taken their job. The problem here is that it is tremendously menial and dependent on effective human judgement to take efficiently evaluate whether the job skill required are a good fit.

Many companies utilises a skill framework when evaluating the suitability of job applicants to the job role. The ideal scenario would be for the applicant to bring in their skills and knowledge with comparison to the company’s framework.



Why ACM?

Getting ACM will align your current knowledge to desired job roles with a detailed gap analysis such that if you have graduated or are the working professionals, you can seek external courses to get the needed skills to reduce the gap, and if you are still a student you can map the courses you need to take to give you the skills and knowledge to get the desired job roles.
